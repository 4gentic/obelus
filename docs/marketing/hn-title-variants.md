# HN title variants

Three "Show HN" titles, each under 80 characters. Pick one on launch morning.

## Privacy-led

**Show HN: Obelus – review papers in the browser, PDF bytes never leave the device**

Rationale: leads with the property HN traditionally scrutinizes first. Names the tool, names the concrete guarantee, and implicitly sets up the reader to audit the claim — which is what we want.

## Format-agnostic-led

**Show HN: Obelus – annotate a PDF, apply the review to LaTeX, Markdown, or Typst source**

Rationale: leads with the verb the tool performs and ends with the thing most reviewers do not expect — that the same bundle round-trips through three source formats. Works well for an audience that has felt the pain of tooling that assumes LaTeX only.

## Self-review-led

**Show HN: Obelus – a reviewer's margin for papers your model mostly wrote**

Rationale: leads with the inverted thesis — you are the reviewer of your own AI-assisted draft — and names the tool's conceptual anchor (the marginal obelus mark). Most editorial of the three; best if the first two feel too feature-list.
